Clinical Trial Invoices

Streamline clinical trial expense processing with intelligent document analysis

Challenge

Clinical trial operations generate complex invoices and expense reports from multiple vendors, sites, and investigators. Manual processing of these documents is time-consuming, error-prone, and can delay trial timelines and budget management. Pharmaceutical companies and CROs need efficient ways to process and validate clinical trial expenses.

Solution

DocRouter automates the processing of clinical trial invoices and expense reports, providing accurate data extraction and validation for better budget management and regulatory compliance.

Invoice Processing

Extract line items, amounts, and vendor information from complex clinical trial invoices

Expense Report Analysis

Process investigator and site expense reports with detailed categorization

Budget Tracking

Regulatory Compliance
