Detailed Use Case

A specialty insurance wholesaler processes handwritten and typed insurance applications submitted via email by producers. These applications contain critical customer information that must be accurately extracted and transferred to their agency management system (AMS) for policy processing and customer identification.

Document Processing

DocRouter.AI processes insurance applications using advanced OCR for handwritten content and language models for data extraction. The system flags low-confidence fields for human review before transferring data to the AMS.

  • Policy details and coverage requirements
  • Risk assessment data and checkboxes
  • Handwritten notes and special instructions
  • Producer and agency information
  • Medical and financial information
